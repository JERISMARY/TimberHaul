Premium Wood Species

Discover the unique character and properties of our finest wood species

Teak
🌏 Myanmar, India
Very Hard
King of tropical hardwoods. Natural oils resist water, insects, and weathering. Perfect for outdoor and marine use.
Oak
🌍 Europe, USA
Hard
Classic European hardwood with beautiful medullary rays. Ideal for flooring, furniture, and wine barrels.
Mahogany
🌎 South America
Medium-Hard
Prized for its deep reddish-brown color and straight grain. The wood of choice for fine furniture and cabinetry.
Pine
🌲 Scandinavia, NZ
Soft
Versatile softwood with bright, clean grain. Economical choice for construction framing and DIY projects.
Walnut
🌎 North America
Hard
Rare dark chocolate grain with striking figuring. The pinnacle of luxury furniture making. Ages beautifully.
Rosewood
🌏 India, Brazil
Very Hard
Prized for its deep purple-rose hue and heavenly scent. Used in luxury furniture and musical instruments.
